Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with yout choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filligs: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beats the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick fryind pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the buTTer is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Titl the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional)When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fols and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook fot another minute, the slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
